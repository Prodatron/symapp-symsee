1000-Introduction


INTRODUCTION TO SYMSEE

SymSee is a picture viewer for SymbOS, which allows you to watch your image libraries in a comfortable way. You can step through your gallery with one mouse or keyboard click for each picture.

Pictures can be viewed in window or fullscreen mode. SymbOS SGX files of any resolutions in 4 or 16 colours, raw 16K CPC files and OCP Art Studio (compressed or uncompressed) screens and are supported. Additionally SymSee supports a slide show feature, where the pictures will change automatically after a special time. A random order is possible as well.

320x200 (CPC, EP) and 512x212 (MSX, PCW; 4 or 16 colours) standard SymbOS SGX files can be used as desktop background pictures directly in SymSee.

1100-Feature overview


FEATURES

- shows 4 and 16 colour pictures in SymbOS graphic format
- shows raw CPC 16K pictures
- shows pictures in OCP Art Studio format (compressed and uncompressed)
- displays pictures of any resolution (e.g. 1280 x 720), only limited by the available amount of RAM memory
- slide show
- displays pictures in (nearly) fullscreen mode
- can configure picture as SymbOS desktop background graphic, if format fits
